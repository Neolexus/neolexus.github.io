Legal & Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Onayi Africa ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your data when you use our website or engage with our luxury travel curation services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: When you make an inquiry or booking, we may collect your name, contact details (email, phone, address), passport/ID, travel preferences, emergency contacts, and payment information.
  • Travel Preferences: We may collect information about your dietary, medical, and accessibility needs to personalize your experience.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our website, including IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalize and curate your travel experiences
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Communicate with you about your trip, updates, and offers
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Improve our services and website

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with trusted partners and suppliers (such as accommodation providers, guides, and transport companies) when necessary to deliver your experience. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. International Data Transfers

As an international travel curator, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your own. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information.

5. Security

We implement physical, administrative, and technical safeguards to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Withdraw your consent (where applicable)

To exercise your rights, please contact us at curator@anoqi.africa.

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on this page.
