Sacred Spirit Charms

Each journey includes an authentic spiritual charm, crafted by village healers and presented in ceremonial leather pouches.

01

Eyes of the Ancestors

Sacred Ingredients:

Burnt kola nut ash, baboon root, river dust

Spiritual Purpose:

Sharpens "spirit vision" with ancestral permission

"They say this charm opens eyes that were never meant to open. Smear the ash lightly under each eye. If the spirits know your name, they may reveal something in the shadows."
Not everyone is meant to see. Use with respect. The eyes of the ancestors are always watching.
02

The Silent Snake

Sacred Ingredients:

Powdered python scale, copper thread, palm resin

Spiritual Purpose:

Protection from snake spirits and wealth curses

"The snake may slither beside you, but it will not bite—if it recognizes this scent. Carry it at night. Especially near rivers or markets where the greedy spirits roam."
Never show this charm to strangers. Its power diminishes with disbelief.
03

Ashes of Vanishing

Sacred Ingredients:

Charcoal from Nyau dancer fires, ghost bean husks

Spiritual Purpose:

Hides the wearer from spirit view in sacred places

"Nyau dancers smear this before they vanish into bottles or fire. Rub it on your wrist before crossing sacred places. If a presence passes, it may skip over you."
Do not use near burial sites. The ancestors may consider it disrespectful.
04

Dream Caller

Sacred Ingredients:

Moonflower petals, sleep bark, ancestor's root

Spiritual Purpose:

Invites ancestral spirits into dreams for communication

"Boil into tea or place under your pillow. Do not speak before sleeping. If the spirit visits, ask only once: 'What do you want me to know?'"
Never use more than three nights consecutively. The door between worlds must remain balanced.
