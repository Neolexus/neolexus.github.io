Rhino Chase Safari

An extreme safari experience that puts you in the heart of a rhino chase through the African bush as part of a wildlife conservation effort. The goal is to track and observe rhinos in their natural habitat, ensuring their safety and studying their behaviors. You can experience the thrill of high-speed safaris while learning about rhino conservation efforts. This high-stakes adventure combines speed with wildlife conservation, blending excitement with ecotourism.

The Summit's Secret: A Night in the Crater's Embrace

Most people stand on the summit for a moment. You will stay for the night. This is not a standard trek; it's a privileged, once-in-a-lifetime expedition for those who want to truly conquer Kilimanjaro. After a successful summit, your team will continue to a private, high-altitude camp situated on the desolate, breathtaking crater rim.
We've mastered the impossible: a night of comfort at nearly 19,000 feet. Your private tent is equipped with a personal hyperbaric chamber to ensure your rest is as restorative as your journey. Witness a surreal sunset over the clouds, a starlit night in the shadow of a volcano, and a sunrise from the top of the world. This isn't just an overnight stay; it's a profound, once-in-a-lifetime experience that redefines what's possible on the world's highest free-standing mountain.
